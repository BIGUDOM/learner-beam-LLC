Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a wide variety of payment methods including credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), bank transfers, and popular e-wallets like Skrill and Neteller. For cryptocurrency deposits, we accept Bitcoin, Ethereum, and USDT.

Are there any hidden fees?

No, we believe in complete transparency. There are no hidden fees or charges. The only fees you'll encounter are the standard spreads on trades, which are clearly displayed on our platform. All our pricing plans have zero commission fees.

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time by making an additional deposit to meet the minimum requirement of the higher tier. Downgrades are also possible when your account balance falls below the current tier's minimum, but we'll notify you before any changes take effect.

How long do withdrawals take?

Withdrawal processing times depend on your account tier. Basic accounts typically process within 3-5 business days, Gold accounts within 1-2 business days, and Platinum accounts enjoy same-day processing for requests submitted before 2 PM EST.

Is my money safe with you?

Absolutely. Client funds are held in segregated accounts with top-tier banks, completely separate from company operational funds. We're regulated by international financial authorities and employ enterprise-grade security including SSL encryption and DDoS protection.

Do you offer a demo account?

Yes, we offer a free demo account with $10,000 in virtual funds so you can practice trading strategies and familiarize yourself with our platform without any risk. The demo account has all the features of a live account and can be accessed immediately after registration.
